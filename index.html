About Me

I'm from a small town named Darien located in Connecticut about 45 minutes from New York City. I've always loved problem solving and efficiency. Whether its something as simple as figuring out if its faster to soap my body and then wash my hair or vice versa or as complex as trying to figure out was the simplest and fastest way is to create an algorithm, I've always loved figuring things out. I first gained a passion for computer science junior year of high school, and I've loved it ever since. I have a special interest in machine learning and artifical intelligence. So far I've completed one course at Miami University in machine learning and am currently working on a personal project to futher enhance my skills.
